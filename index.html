Recipe :

Chicken Biryani is a highly aromatic, mouth-watering staple dish that needs no introduction. Because of its endless nuanced flavors, it is perhaps the most comforting meal of Indian cuisine that has been a part of community feasts, family gatherings, and almost every Indian household for ages. Packed with fragrant rice and juicy, tender chicken pieces, it is a perfect one-pot dinner recipe that your whole family will love

Incredients :

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Ginger and Garlic paste
  • Fresh herbs
  • Milk and Saffron
  • Yogurt and Lime juice
  • Ghee + Oil
  • Onions

    • Preparation :

    1.In a pan at medium heat, add some oil and cook the onions for 10 minutes. Add garlic ginger paste and fry for 2 minutes before adding the chicken pieces. Cook for 2 -3 minutes at high heat.

    2.Once the chicken changes in color, add in the biryani mix, give it a good stir. Splash in some water, place in the tomatoes and mix.

    3.Pour in the yogurt and give it a mix. Sprinkle in coriander and green chillies. Cover and cook for 20 minutes. Let it cook till the sauce has almost dried up. Turn off the heat and set aside. For the rice; In a pot of boiling water add some salt, cinnamon sticks, and cloves. Mix till salt dissolves. Add in the basmati rice and let this cook till its done.

    4.Cover and cook for 7 - 8 minutes. Mix to make sure the rice is coated with the sauce. Transfer to a plate and serve garnished with lemon slices.
